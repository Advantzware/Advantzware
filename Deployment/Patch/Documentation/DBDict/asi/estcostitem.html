ASI.estCostItem

The estimate calculation for the saleable item.ÿ This differs from the blank in that there can be multiple blanks on a given estimate that share the same part number (eg. small items that fit on on multiple combo forms).ÿ The costs on this are a sum of all blank costs for a given estimate.ÿ The estItem will hold the official "order" related estimate calculations like freight, warehousing, commission, and target pricing.
